How I Earned ₹50,000-60,000/Month With Custom T-Shirt Printing — My Real 2018 Blueprint 🔥

2017 mein printer ₹10 lakh se neeche nahi milte the. Aaj ₹20,000-
50,000 mein mil jaate hain. Sab aasan ho gaya.

Ab AUDIENCE kaise laao? (Ye asli trick hai):
Maine 2017-18 mein target kiya tha — DELHI UNIVERSITY wale log.
→ Unke Facebook/Instagram pages ko engage kiya (comment, like)
→ DU se related designs banayi, share ki
→ Aisi requirement start ki jo unki zaroorat thi
→ Wo log mujhse kaam karwana start ho gaye

Tum bhi ek NICHE pakdo (maine DU pakda tha):
→ Us niche ke Instagram/Facebook pages pe active raho
→ Related videos banao
→ 10-12 din mein wo log tumhe notice karenge, dhundh ke aayenge

CONSISTENCY zaroori hai:
Agar 10 din mein result na aaye, 15 din mein chhodo mat. Karte raho.
Mujhe bhi 3-4 mahine lage the. Kaam 10-12 din ka hai, bas consistency chahiye.

Ye ₹50-60K/month wala income tumhe STABLE kar dega — dusra paisa ka
source dhundhne ki zaroorat nahi padegi.

Main ab ye sab nahi karta (ab wholesale karta hoon), isliye tumhe
khul ke bata pa raha hoon. Ye mera asli experience hai.
